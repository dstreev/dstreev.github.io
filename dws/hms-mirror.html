hms-mirror

"hms-mirror" is a utility used to bridge the gap between two clusters and migrate hive metadata.
HMS-Mirror is distributed under the APLv2 license.

The application will migrate hive metastore data (metadata) between two clusters. You can configure and implement several different 'Data Strategies' in hms-mirror to accomplish various migration scenarios.
